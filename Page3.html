❇ Reviews Of Nalanda Mahila College

Academics: Offers undergraduate programs in Arts, Science, and Vocational courses (like BCA). Faculty are described as knowledgeable and supportive, with course delivery receiving a 3.5/5 rating.
Campus & Infrastructure: Features a clean, green, and disciplined environment. Basic amenities include ventilated classrooms, a well-stocked library, and adequate science/computer laboratories.
Value for Money: Highly appreciated for its affordability and value for money, making higher education accessible.
